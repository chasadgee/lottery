Registration & Account

To participate, users must create an account by providing accurate personal information. Registration allows you to purchase tickets, view results, track entries, and manage winnings securely through your dashboard.

How do I play the lottery online?

Log in to your account, choose your preferred lottery game, select your numbers or use the quick pick option, and complete the ticket purchase. Your entry is automatically confirmed and recorded in your account.

Responsible Gaming

We encourage responsible gaming for entertainment only. Play within your budget, set personal limits, and avoid chasing losses. If gaming becomes a concern, please seek professional help or use available self-control tools.

Lottery Scams

We never request payments, passwords, or personal details via email, SMS, or social media. All official notifications are available only through your registered account. Report any suspicious activity to our support team immediately.
